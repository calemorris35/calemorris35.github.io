Seasons

Spring

X

Spring begins in march every year, in 2020 it begins on March 19th. The beginning of spring is usually still cold but as the days pass, it heats up. Flowers begin to bloom, animals begin to come out of hibernation, and many people celebrate easter in the spring. Spring often represents the beginning of a new year because, after a long cold winter, life begins to pick up again.

Summer

x

Summer begins in June every year, and in 2020 it begins on June 20th. The beginning of summer is when the temperatures start to rise and continues to get warmer and warmer as the season goes on. People start to spend more time doing activities outdoors in summer. Many people celebrate independence day during the summer and labor day often marks the end of summer because children return to school.

Fall

x

Fall begins in September every year and in 2020 it begins on September 22nd. The beginning of every fall the temperature starts to drop until winter starts. The leaves begin to change color as the trees and grass slowly die. Many people celebrate holidays such as halloween and thanksgiving during fall. Its common to see people wearing scarves and boots as the temperature drops.
